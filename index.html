The Discipline

Three rituals,
one craft.

We do a small number of things, slowly and well. Every visit is composed — each cut intentional, each line measured, each silhouette resolved before we leave.

01

Hedge Trimming

Sharp lines, clean planes, deliberate shape. We trim, taper, and re-establish architectural form on boxwood, privet, holly, yew, and hornbeam — restoring symmetry and crisp shoulders before they soften.

Formal hedgingTopiary maintenanceGeometric shapingRenovation cuts
02

Mini Tree Cutting

Specimen care for ornamentals, espaliers, and small canopies under twenty feet. Selective limbing, crown thinning, deadwood removal — preserving the tree's natural gesture while improving light, airflow, and longevity.

Ornamental pruningCrown thinningDeadwoodingEspalier
03

Full-Site Cleanup

End-of-season restoration. We edge beds, lift debris, refresh mulch, clear gutters of leaf-fall, blow drives clean, and leave the property reset — quiet, sharp, and ready for what's next.

Bed edgingLeaf removalMulch refreshEstate reset
The Studio

Landscaping,
as composition.

Apex Grounds is a small, deliberate studio. No subcontracting, no rushed visits, no cookie-cutter packages — only the same crew, returning to the same property, year after year.

We were trained by horticulturalists, classical gardeners, and one stubborn mentor who measured every hedge with a string line. That discipline never left. We approach a property the way an editor approaches a manuscript — restraint first, then precision, then polish.

A garden isn't a chore list. It's a portrait of the people who live in it — and we treat it that way.
  • Owner-led visits — your property is never handed off.
  • Fully insured, licensed, background-checked crew.
  • Hand tools first; power tools only when warranted.
  • Itemized aftercare notes after every visit.

The Method

From walkthrough
to final sweep.

A repeatable, unhurried process — designed so the property is left better than the day before, every single visit.

01

Walkthrough

An on-site visit. We assess condition, listen to your priorities, and document what the property needs in order, not all at once.

02

Composition

You receive a clear, fixed-fee plan — what we'll do, when, and what success looks like. No padded hours, no surprise line items.

03

Execution

Same crew, every time. We arrive when we say, work quietly, protect adjacent plantings, and stop only when the cut is right.

04

Aftercare

A photographed report, a watering note where relevant, and a calendar mark for the next visit — kept on file for the season.
